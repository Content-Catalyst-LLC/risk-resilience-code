# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| monitoring_system_name | Name of the monitoring system, platform, network, dashboard, or observing program |
| jurisdiction | Geographic, administrative, watershed, coastal, urban, ecological, or institutional jurisdiction |
| system_domain | Monitoring domain such as hydrology, air quality, biodiversity, climate services, coastal systems, drought, heat health, early warning, or integrated environmental intelligence |
| observation_coverage_index | Spatial, temporal, and thematic coverage across hazards, ecosystems, infrastructure, and exposed communities |
| data_quality_index | Calibration, validation, completeness, metadata, uncertainty documentation, and reliability |
| timeliness_index | Whether observations are available quickly enough to support warning, intervention, or adaptive management |
| interoperability_index | Whether datasets can be connected across agencies, systems, scales, and domains |
| analytical_capacity_index | Capacity to interpret signals, compare baselines, identify anomalies, and understand risk pathways |
| warning_dissemination_index | Ability to communicate warnings through accessible, trusted, actionable channels |
| community_validation_index | Inclusion of local evidence, lived knowledge, Indigenous knowledge, and community interpretation |
| action_linkage_index | Whether monitoring triggers warnings, maintenance, adaptation, restoration, protection, or policy change |
| rights_safeguard_index | Privacy, consent, data protection, anti-surveillance safeguards, and protections against displacement or misuse |
| blind_spot_index | Unmonitored places, hazards, communities, ecosystems, or exposure pathways |
| uncertainty_burden_index | Missingness, poor calibration, weak confidence, unresolved uncertainty, or model limitations |
| decision_lag_index | Delay between environmental signal detection and institutional response |
| maintenance_risk_index | Risk of sensor failure, data interruption, poor calibration, funding loss, or platform degradation |
| misuse_risk_index | Surveillance, displacement, data extraction, privacy harm, political manipulation, or harmful use of monitoring data |
