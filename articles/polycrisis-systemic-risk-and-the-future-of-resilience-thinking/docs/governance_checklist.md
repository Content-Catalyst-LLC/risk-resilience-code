# Polycrisis Governance Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing a resilience plan, risk register, scenario matrix, stress test, dashboard, or public-sector risk governance process.

## Interaction and Coupling

- Are crisis domains evaluated together rather than separately?
- Are cross-domain pathways documented?
- Are compound shocks and cascading failures included?
- Are slow-moving stresses connected to acute shocks?
- Are common dependencies identified?

## Thresholds and Feedback

- Are ecological, infrastructure, financial, institutional, and social thresholds identified?
- Are early-warning indicators linked to action triggers?
- Are reinforcing feedback loops mapped?
- Are cumulative stress and depletion tracked?

## Equity and Legitimacy

- Are vulnerable groups included in the analysis?
- Are distributional impacts assessed?
- Can affected communities challenge the evidence?
- Are trust, fairness, and legitimacy treated as resilience variables?

## Governance and Learning

- Are owners assigned for cross-domain risks?
- Are adaptive decision pathways defined?
- Are data provenance and auditability documented?
- Are plans updated after shocks, exercises, and new evidence?
- Are maladaptation risks explicitly reviewed?
