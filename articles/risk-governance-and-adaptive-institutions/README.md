# Risk Governance and Adaptive Institutions

This article directory supports the Risk & Resilience article:

**Risk Governance and Adaptive Institutions**

The workflows model how risk governance quality, adaptive institutional capacity, legitimacy, participation, coordination, learning, justice orientation, uncertainty, fragmentation, capture risk, and systemic exposure shape resilience.

## Analytical Focus

The article examines:

- Risk governance quality
- Adaptive institutional capacity
- Anticipatory capacity
- Appraisal quality
- Cross-sector coordination
- Participation and stakeholder engagement
- Transparency and legitimacy
- Learning capacity
- Institutional memory
- Justice orientation
- Monitoring and feedback
- Policy revision capacity
- Institutional fragmentation
- Uncertainty load
- Capture risk
- Systemic risk exposure
- Vulnerability exposure

## Suggested Input Dataset

Expected file:

```text
risk_governance_institutions_panel.csv
```

Each `*_index` field should be normalized from 0 to 1.

Higher values should mean more of the named property. For example:

- `anticipatory_capacity_index`: higher = stronger horizon scanning and early warning
- `coordination_capacity_index`: higher = stronger cross-sector coordination
- `fragmentation_index`: higher = greater institutional fragmentation
- `capture_risk_index`: higher = greater risk of governance capture
