# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| institution_or_system | Name of the institution, governance system, or risk-governance arrangement |
| jurisdiction | Geographic or institutional jurisdiction |
| risk_domain | Risk domain such as climate, public health, finance, cyber, infrastructure, disaster risk, or ecological risk |
| anticipatory_capacity_index | Horizon scanning, early warning, scenario planning, preparedness, and foresight |
| appraisal_quality_index | Scientific appraisal, concern assessment, uncertainty analysis, and risk framing |
| coordination_capacity_index | Cross-sector and cross-scale coordination capacity |
| participation_strength_index | Meaningful stakeholder and community engagement |
| transparency_index | Openness, clarity, documentation, and uncertainty communication |
| legitimacy_index | Trust, accountability, perceived fairness, and public credibility |
| learning_capacity_index | Capacity to learn from feedback, crisis reviews, monitoring, and failure |
| institutional_memory_index | Ability to preserve lessons beyond political and budget cycles |
| justice_orientation_index | Attention to vulnerability, unequal exposure, and distributional harm |
| monitoring_feedback_index | Strength of monitoring systems and feedback channels |
| policy_revision_capacity_index | Ability to revise policy, standards, budgets, and plans when conditions change |
| fragmentation_index | Degree of siloed authority, coordination gaps, or responsibility diffusion |
| uncertainty_load_index | Complexity, ambiguity, and uncertainty pressure on decision-making |
| capture_risk_index | Risk that powerful interests distort governance processes |
| systemic_risk_exposure_index | Exposure to cascading, cross-sector, or transboundary risk |
| vulnerability_exposure_index | Exposure of vulnerable communities, systems, or populations to harm |
