# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| jurisdiction | Geographic or institutional jurisdiction |
| governance_system | Governance system, program, region, institutional arrangement, or cross-sector coordination structure |
| primary_risk_domain | Primary risk domain such as climate adaptation, critical infrastructure, public health, emergency management, water, energy, housing, food systems, cyber resilience, or local governance |
| cross_sector_coordination_index | Strength of coordination across agencies, sectors, operators, and jurisdictions |
| dependency_visibility_index | Quality of mapping for infrastructure, service, supply-chain, digital, labor, ecological, and social dependencies |
| governance_integration_index | Degree of whole-of-government, multilevel, and cross-institutional integration |
| data_interoperability_index | Ability to share and use evidence across systems securely and accountably |
| public_accountability_index | Transparency, auditability, responsibility, public reporting, and correction capacity |
| justice_equity_index | Degree to which protection, participation, and benefits reach vulnerable groups fairly |
| local_capability_index | Local authority, resources, community knowledge, trust, and implementation capacity |
| adaptive_learning_index | Ability to learn from exercises, disruptions, audits, after-action reviews, and community feedback |
| investment_alignment_index | Degree to which budgets, finance, and investment align across sectors around resilience outcomes |
| fragmentation_risk_index | Risk from institutional silos, disconnected mandates, separate budgets, and weak coordination mechanisms |
| mandate_conflict_index | Risk from contradictory responsibilities, competing incentives, or unresolved authority gaps |
| governance_data_gap_index | Missing, incompatible, inaccessible, stale, or non-contestable evidence |
| maladaptation_risk_index | Risk that one intervention shifts harm or solves one problem by creating another |
| private_operator_opacity_index | Risk from critical private operators, vendors, or infrastructure owners lacking transparency or public accountability |
| accountability_diffusion_index | Risk that many actors are involved but responsibility is unclear |
