# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| initiative_name | Name of the resilience, adaptation, transition, recovery, restoration, or transformation initiative |
| jurisdiction | Geographic or institutional jurisdiction |
| transformation_domain | Domain such as climate adaptation, disaster recovery, energy transition, housing, migration, infrastructure, ecological repair, public health, food systems, or digital governance |
| technical_resilience_index | Technical capacity to absorb, adapt, recover, or preserve function under stress |
| distributive_justice_index | Fair allocation of protection, benefits, burdens, recovery, and risk reduction |
| procedural_justice_index | Meaningful participation, accessible public information, and influence over decisions |
| recognition_index | Respect for marginalized communities, histories, cultures, identities, and knowledge systems |
| rights_protection_index | Protection of human rights, environmental rights, land rights, information rights, and access to remedy |
| institutional_accountability_index | Transparency, auditability, appeal mechanisms, public reporting, and responsibility for harm |
| ecological_governance_index | Protection of ecosystems, biodiversity, soil, water, climate stability, and living systems |
| intergenerational_responsibility_index | Preservation of future wellbeing, adaptive capacity, ecological conditions, and institutional viability |
| public_legitimacy_index | Trust, fairness, public credibility, and willingness to accept collective decisions |
| data_transparency_index | Quality of evidence, disaggregation, provenance, access, uncertainty disclosure, and contestability |
| maladaptation_risk_index | Risk that the initiative increases vulnerability, locks in harm, or undermines long-term resilience |
| harm_shifting_risk_index | Risk that the initiative transfers risk, cost, loss, or exposure onto less powerful groups |
| exclusion_risk_index | Risk that affected people are excluded from information, participation, services, protection, or recovery |
| coercion_risk_index | Risk of forced, punitive, or non-consensual transformation |
| displacement_risk_index | Risk of physical, economic, cultural, or political displacement |
| unequal_burden_index | Degree to which costs or sacrifices are concentrated on vulnerable or marginalized groups |
