# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| institution_name | Name of the public body, agency, department, ministry, authority, local government, or public institution |
| jurisdiction | Geographic or institutional jurisdiction |
| institution_type | Type of public institution such as national ministry, local government, agency, regulator, authority, emergency body, public-health institution, or service provider |
| primary_service_domain | Main service domain such as emergency management, public health, social protection, finance, procurement, housing, water, transport, education, or digital public administration |
| anticipatory_foresight_index | Strategic foresight, horizon scanning, scenario planning, and preparedness capacity |
| continuity_operations_index | Continuity planning for essential public functions and services |
| administrative_capacity_index | Staffing, authority, training, data quality, institutional memory, and operational capability |
| coordination_capacity_index | Capacity to coordinate across agencies, sectors, levels of government, operators, and communities |
| risk_informed_finance_index | Budgeting, investment, contingency funding, maintenance, and fiscal tools aligned with risk |
| procurement_resilience_index | Supply continuity, emergency procurement, vendor risk management, transparency, and public value |
| digital_fallback_index | Cybersecurity, manual alternatives, offline access, accessible channels, and continuity if digital systems fail |
| public_legitimacy_index | Trust, communication, transparency, credibility, and public confidence |
| justice_service_equity_index | Equitable access, rights protection, inclusive service delivery, and protection of vulnerable groups |
| learning_adaptation_index | Evaluation, stress testing, after-action review, feedback, and institutional revision |
| fragmentation_risk_index | Risk from silos, disconnected plans, unclear roles, and weak coordination |
| underinvestment_risk_index | Risk from chronic underfunding of prevention, maintenance, staffing, preparedness, and public capacity |
| staffing_fragility_index | Risk from burnout, turnover, inadequate training, vacancies, and loss of institutional memory |
| digital_dependency_risk_index | Risk from brittle digital reliance, cloud dependency, cyber exposure, or lack of offline alternatives |
| procurement_vulnerability_index | Risk from supplier concentration, poor contract resilience, supply disruption, or weak emergency procurement controls |
| accountability_gap_index | Weak transparency, unclear responsibility, poor appeal mechanisms, weak remedies, and non-contestable decisions |
