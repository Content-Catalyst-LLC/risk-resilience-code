# Public Institutional Resilience Strategy

This article directory supports the Risk & Resilience article:

**Public Institutional Resilience Strategy**

Published URL:

```text
https://sustainablecatalyst.com/resilience-strategy-in-public-institutions/
```

The workflows model how public institutions build resilience through anticipatory foresight, continuity of operations, administrative capacity, coordination, risk-informed finance, resilient procurement, digital fallback capacity, public legitimacy, justice and service equity, institutional learning, and adaptation.

## Analytical Focus

The article examines:

- Public institutional resilience strategy
- Strategic foresight and anticipatory capacity
- Continuity of core public functions
- Administrative capacity under stress
- Coordination across levels of government
- Risk-informed finance and budgeting
- Resilient procurement
- Supply continuity
- Digital public infrastructure
- Manual fallback capacity
- Public legitimacy and trust
- Public communication
- Justice and service equity
- Institutional learning and adaptation
- Crisis preparedness and public-sector resilience

## Suggested Input Dataset

Expected file:

```text
public_institutional_resilience_panel.csv
```

Each `*_index` field should be normalized from 0 to 1.

Higher values should mean more of the named property. For example:

- `anticipatory_foresight_index`: higher = stronger foresight and scenario capacity
- `continuity_operations_index`: higher = stronger continuity planning for core public functions
- `digital_dependency_risk_index`: higher = greater risk from brittle digital dependence
- `accountability_gap_index`: higher = weaker transparency, remedy, and responsibility
