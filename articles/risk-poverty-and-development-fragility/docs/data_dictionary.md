# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| place_name | Name of the region, city, district, settlement, community, or planning unit |
| jurisdiction | Geographic, administrative, or institutional jurisdiction |
| place_type | Place category such as fragile context, rural district, informal settlement, conflict-affected region, climate-exposed community, or under-served urban area |
| risk_exposure_index | Exposure to climate, conflict, price, disease, displacement, economic, ecological, or service-disruption shocks |
| multidimensional_poverty_index | Overlapping deprivation in health, education, housing, sanitation, energy, food security, and living standards |
| institutional_weakness_index | Weak public capacity, low legitimacy, corruption, limited administrative reach, poor coordination, or weak service delivery |
| livelihood_precarity_index | Insecure work, income volatility, informal work, climate-sensitive livelihoods, and lack of worker protection |
| service_deficit_index | Gaps in health, education, water, sanitation, energy, transport, communications, and public administration |
| conflict_pressure_index | Insecurity, violence, coercion, disruption, and conflict-related risk |
| climate_stress_index | Heat, drought, flood, storms, crop stress, water scarcity, sea-level rise, and other climate-related pressure |
| displacement_pressure_index | Forced movement, migration pressure, housing loss, eviction, conflict displacement, or climate displacement risk |
| social_protection_index | Access to cash transfers, food assistance, unemployment support, health coverage, child benefits, pensions, disability support, and disaster-responsive safety nets |
| household_buffer_index | Savings, assets, secure housing, food reserves, care networks, insurance, and coping capacity |
| adaptive_capacity_index | Ability to prepare, respond, recover, learn, and adjust before future shocks worsen |
| service_continuity_index | Ability of basic services to continue during stress |
| institutional_trust_index | Legitimacy, fairness, responsiveness, and confidence in public institutions |
| community_voice_index | Participation, representation, organizing capacity, local knowledge, and influence over decisions |
