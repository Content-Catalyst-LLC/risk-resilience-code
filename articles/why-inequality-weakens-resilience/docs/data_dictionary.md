# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| place_name | Name of the region, city, settlement, district, community, or planning unit |
| jurisdiction | Geographic, administrative, or institutional jurisdiction |
| place_type | Place category such as city, region, rural district, informal settlement, peripheral territory, fragile context, or under-served community |
| system_capacity_index | Aggregate institutional, fiscal, infrastructure, and social capacity |
| distributed_protection_index | Degree to which public protection reaches vulnerable groups and under-served places |
| household_buffer_index | Household coping capacity such as savings, secure housing, food access, care, insurance, and informal support |
| service_access_index | Practical access to health, water, sanitation, energy, transport, education, emergency systems, and public services |
| institutional_trust_index | Public legitimacy, responsiveness, fairness, and confidence in institutions |
| adaptive_capacity_index | Ability to prepare, adjust, recover, and reduce future risk |
| social_protection_index | Access to cash support, unemployment support, food assistance, pensions, disability support, health coverage, and disaster assistance |
| community_voice_index | Participation, representation, organizing capacity, local knowledge, and influence over decisions |
| exposure_concentration_index | Degree to which hazards are concentrated among disadvantaged groups or places |
| multidimensional_deprivation_index | Overlapping deficits in health, education, housing, living standards, security, and opportunity |
| social_exclusion_index | Exclusion from institutions, services, finance, data systems, legal recognition, or political voice |
| recovery_inequality_index | Unequal recovery speed, compensation, service restoration, and long-term scarring after shocks |
| digital_exclusion_index | Lack of digital access, devices, literacy, connectivity, electricity, accessible design, or digital trust |
| fiscal_stress_index | Fiscal pressure limiting public investment, maintenance, adaptation, and recovery capacity |
