# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| boundary_name | Name of the planetary boundary or Earth-system process |
| earth_system_domain | Domain such as climate, biosphere, land, freshwater, nutrients, oceans, atmosphere, chemicals, or ozone |
| boundary_status | Status such as breached, high risk, close to breach, safe zone, or regional exceedance |
| boundary_transgression_index | Degree to which the boundary is beyond safer operating conditions |
| pressure_trend_index | Direction and pace of worsening pressure |
| interaction_strength_index | Degree to which this boundary reinforces or is reinforced by other boundary pressures |
| reversibility_risk_index | Likelihood that damage becomes difficult, slow, or impossible to reverse |
| human_system_exposure_index | Exposure of food, water, health, infrastructure, finance, migration, and governance systems to boundary disruption |
| monitoring_confidence_index | Strength of monitoring, measurement, data quality, and scientific confidence |
| adaptive_capacity_index | Social, institutional, technological, ecological, and community capacity to reduce harm and adjust |
| governance_quality_index | Coordination, accountability, legitimacy, enforcement, public participation, and ability to govern across sectors and scales |
| justice_transition_index | Degree to which response pathways protect rights, livelihoods, vulnerable communities, and future generations |
| policy_response_index | Strength of policy, regulation, financing, implementation, and system transformation response |
