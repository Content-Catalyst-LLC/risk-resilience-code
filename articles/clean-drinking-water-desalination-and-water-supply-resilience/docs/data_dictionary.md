# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| water_system_name | Name of the drinking-water system, utility, desalination portfolio, small system, rural supply, or planning unit |
| jurisdiction | Geographic, utility, municipal, regional, tribal, island, state, or national jurisdiction |
| system_type | System category such as urban utility, rural system, small system, desalination portfolio, reuse portfolio, island system, or emergency supply system |
| source_protection_index | Watershed, aquifer, reservoir, recharge-zone, and source-water protection |
| treatment_capacity_index | Ability to remove or inactivate relevant pathogens, salts, metals, chemicals, and contaminants |
| distribution_reliability_index | Reliability of pipes, pressure, pumps, tanks, storage, service lines, and point-of-use delivery |
| monitoring_quality_index | Water-quality testing, sensors, laboratory capacity, sampling design, data transparency, and early warning |
| supply_diversity_index | Diversity of surface water, groundwater, desalination, reuse, emergency storage, interconnections, and demand management |
| energy_resilience_index | Backup power, renewable integration, grid reliability, fuel planning, and energy security for water operations |
| affordability_index | Whether households can access safe water without unaffordable burden |
| governance_capacity_index | Regulation, finance, staffing, operator capacity, public communication, emergency planning, and trust |
| contamination_risk_index | Microbial, chemical, industrial, agricultural, geological, distribution-system, or household-storage risk |
| infrastructure_aging_index | Aging pipes, pumps, tanks, service lines, treatment plants, and control systems |
| salinity_pressure_index | Seawater intrusion, brackish groundwater, salinized sources, and dissolved-solids pressure |
| energy_dependence_index | Dependence on electricity or fuels for treatment, pumping, desalination, reuse, monitoring, and distribution |
| brine_burden_index | Environmental and governance burden of desalination concentrate and brine disposal |
| access_inequality_index | Unequal service, safety, affordability, distance, reliability, and political voice |
