# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| jurisdiction_or_portfolio | Name of the country, city, region, public portfolio, insurance pool, or investment portfolio |
| risk_domain | Risk domain such as climate, flood, wildfire, public health, infrastructure, agriculture, cyber, or sovereign disaster risk |
| sector | Sector such as households, public assets, infrastructure, agriculture, insurance, sovereign finance, housing, or small business |
| risk_visibility_index | Strength of risk data, disclosure, mapping, stress testing, and financial-stability surveillance |
| insurance_coverage_index | Breadth and reliability of insurance or pooled risk coverage |
| prearranged_finance_index | Availability of finance arranged before disaster or systemic shock |
| fiscal_capacity_index | Public fiscal space, reserves, budget flexibility, and emergency financing capacity |
| resilience_investment_index | Investment in risk reduction, adaptation, infrastructure, ecosystems, housing, public health, and preparedness |
| mitigation_incentive_index | Degree to which financial systems reward risk reduction and discourage maladaptation |
| equity_protection_index | Protection for vulnerable households, communities, public services, and countries |
| insurance_affordability_index | Affordability and accessibility of insurance or financial protection |
| public_risk_pool_capacity_index | Strength of public risk pools, pooled insurance, or public-private risk sharing |
| contingent_credit_capacity_index | Availability of contingent credit lines or emergency liquidity |
| catastrophe_bond_capacity_index | Availability and suitability of catastrophe bonds or insurance-linked securities |
| social_protection_capacity_index | Ability of social protection systems to absorb shocks and protect vulnerable people |
| disclosure_quality_index | Quality, comparability, completeness, and usefulness of risk disclosure |
| protection_gap_index | Degree of uninsured or unfinanced expected loss exposure |
| debt_stress_index | Fiscal pressure, borrowing constraints, or debt vulnerability after shocks |
| hidden_exposure_index | Undisclosed, mispriced, or poorly understood exposure |
| systemic_transmission_risk_index | Risk that financial losses propagate across institutions, sectors, or public budgets |
| maladaptation_risk_index | Risk that financing arrangements reinforce exposure rather than reduce it |
