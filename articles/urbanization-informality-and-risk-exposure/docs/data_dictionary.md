# Data Dictionary

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| settlement_name | Name of the settlement, neighborhood, district, or planning unit |
| city | City or metropolitan area |
| country_or_region | Country, region, or jurisdiction |
| settlement_type | Settlement category such as informal settlement, under-serviced neighborhood, peripheral growth area, floodplain settlement, heat-exposed district, or mixed urban settlement |
| flood_exposure_index | Exposure to river, pluvial, coastal, drainage, or stormwater flooding |
| heat_exposure_index | Exposure to urban heat, heat islands, poor ventilation, and heat-sensitive conditions |
| landslide_or_fire_exposure_index | Exposure to landslide, slope instability, fire, or related hazards |
| housing_vulnerability_index | Structural, thermal, overcrowding, material, or safety vulnerability of housing |
| infrastructure_deficit_index | Deficit in drainage, roads, electricity, water, sanitation, waste, health, emergency access, or other infrastructure |
| service_access_index | Practical access to water, sanitation, health, energy, transport, waste, emergency support, and other services |
| tenure_security_index | Security of occupancy, anti-eviction protection, recognition, and ability to invest safely in housing |
| livelihood_precarity_index | Income instability, informal work dependence, asset fragility, and exposure to work disruption |
| social_protection_access_index | Access to cash support, insurance, public benefits, recovery assistance, worker protection, and safety nets |
| community_adaptation_index | Strength of community-led adaptation, local organization, savings groups, mutual aid, mapping, and local preparedness |
| institutional_protection_index | Public recognition, upgrading support, emergency access, rights protection, investment, and accountability |
| climate_stress_index | Current and future climate-related pressure affecting the settlement |
| displacement_pressure_index | Risk of eviction, forced relocation, green displacement, redevelopment pressure, or speculative displacement |
| data_visibility_index | Degree to which the settlement is visible in official maps, data systems, planning records, and public investment processes |
