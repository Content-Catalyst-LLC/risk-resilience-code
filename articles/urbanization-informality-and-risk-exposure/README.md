# Urbanization, Informality, and Risk Exposure

This article directory supports the Risk & Resilience article:

**Urbanization, Informality, and Risk Exposure**

The workflows model how flood exposure, heat exposure, landslide or fire exposure, housing vulnerability, infrastructure deficits, service access, tenure security, livelihood precarity, social protection, community adaptation, institutional protection, climate stress, displacement pressure, and data visibility shape urban risk exposure and informal-settlement protection gaps.

## Analytical Focus

The article examines:

- Urbanization as risk production
- Informality as exclusionary urbanization
- Informal settlements
- Risk exposure
- Unequal urban protection
- Land markets and housing precarity
- Tenure insecurity
- Service and infrastructure deficits
- Heat and flood vulnerability
- Informal livelihoods and thin buffers
- Community-led adaptation
- Settlement upgrading
- Infrastructure finance
- Protection gaps
- Data visibility and mapping ethics
- Inclusive urban resilience

## Suggested Input Dataset

Expected file:

```text
urban_informality_risk_exposure_panel.csv
```

Each `*_index` field should be normalized from 0 to 1.

Higher values should mean more of the named property.
